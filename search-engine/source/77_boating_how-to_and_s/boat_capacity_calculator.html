    About this tool

    This calculator uses common small-boat guidelines and your selected water type to suggest a conservative passenger capacity. It is intended as a planning aid for new boaters.

    • Always obey your boat's official capacity plate and local regulations.
    • Reduce passenger count in poor weather or rough water.
    • Distribute weight evenly and keep heavy gear low in the boat.

    Capacity safety tips

    • Count people, not just seats. Children and adults still count as full passengers.
    • Account for fuel, batteries, coolers, and gear when estimating total weight.
    • Stay well under the maximum in unfamiliar waters or at night.
    • Ask guests to stay seated when underway to avoid sudden shifts in weight.

1. Overview

Magical Revue Booking is an online service that helps people discover, compare, and book magic shows and related entertainment for events such as kids birthdays, corporate events, school shows, and more.

This privacy policy explains how we handle personal information when you browse our site, create custom packages, request quotes, contact performers, purchase gift cards, or place bookings. It also explains your choices and rights, and how to contact us with privacy requests.

When we say personal information, we mean any information that identifies or can reasonably be linked to you, such as your name, contact details, booking history, or event details.

2. Information we collect

We collect information in three main ways: information you provide directly, information we receive from your use of the site, and information related to payments and gift cards.

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account and contact details. If you create an account or complete a booking, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Booking and event details. When you book a show or request a quote, we may collect event type, event date and time, venue address, guest count, preferred performer or show, and any notes you provide. This information is stored in our Booking and related records.
  • Performer messages. When you send a PerformerContactMessage to a performer, we collect your name, email address, event details you choose to share, and the content of your message.
  • Custom packages. If you use our Build Your Own Show flow, we collect the options you choose (such as BaseShowOption and AddOnOption selections), event details, and any custom package name you provide.
  • Gift cards. When purchasing a gift card, we collect sender and recipient details needed to deliver the card, such as names, email addresses, delivery method, and your message.
  • Customer support. If you contact us for help, we collect the content of your message and any contact details you share.

2.2 Information from your use of the site

  • Usage data. We may collect information about how you browse and interact with the site, such as pages viewed, search queries, and navigation patterns. This helps us understand which shows or features are most useful.
  • Device and log data. Our systems may automatically record basic technical details such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and the date and time of your requests.
  • Cookies and similar technologies. As described later in this policy, we may use cookies or similar tools to remember your preferences (for example, saved favorites or search filters) and to understand site performance.

2.3 Payment and transaction information

  • Booking and cart details. When you confirm a booking or purchase gift cards, we store details about what you purchased, prices, discounts, and total amounts. This information is associated with your Booking, Cart, and CartItem records.
  • Payment processing. We work with payment processors to handle card payments securely. Card details are typically handled directly by the processor and are not stored in our own systems. We may receive limited information back from the processor, such as the last few digits of your card, card type, and a payment status.

3. How we use your information

We use personal information to provide and improve our services, to communicate with you, and to meet our legal and contractual obligations. Typical uses include:

  • Providing the site and services. For example, using Booking, Show, Performer, and ShowPackage data to display availability, calculate prices, and manage your reservations.
  • Processing bookings and payments. To confirm your bookings, issue booking references, apply PromoCodes, and send confirmations and receipts.
  • Facilitating communication. To send messages about your booking or quote request, to forward PerformerContactMessages to performers, and to send service emails such as reminders or updates.
  • Personalising your experience. To remember what you have viewed or saved (such as FavoritesList items), and to suggest relevant shows or packages.
  • Customer support. To respond to your questions, troubleshoot issues, and improve our support services.
  • Security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance. To protect our systems and users, to prevent misuse, and to meet obligations such as tax, accounting, or regulatory requirements.
  • Marketing and service updates. To send information about new shows, special offers, or changes to our services, where permitted by law and your communication preferences.

4. Data storage and security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information we hold. These measures are designed to reduce the risk of accidental loss, unauthorised access, or misuse of personal information.

  • We restrict access to personal information to staff and service providers who need it to perform their work.
  • We use authentication and access controls on internal systems that store Booking, CustomPackage, GiftCardOption, and related records.
  • Where possible, we use secure network connections and industry standard encryption technologies for data in transit.

No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. If we become aware of a security incident that affects your personal information, we will review the impact and, where required by law, notify you and any relevant authorities.

5. Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as we have a valid reason to do so, including providing the services you request, managing our relationship with you, meeting legal obligations, and resolving disputes.

  • Bookings and transactions. Booking, Cart, and payment related records are generally kept for the period required by tax and accounting rules, which may be several years.
  • Performer and quote messages. PerformerContactMessage and QuoteRequest records may be kept for as long as necessary to manage the conversation, respond to follow up questions, and improve our services.
  • Favorites and compare lists. FavoritesList and CompareList data may be kept while your account is active or until you clear these items.
  • Marketing preferences. Records of your subscription status or opt out choices may be stored so we can respect your preferences.

When we no longer need personal information, we will either delete it or anonymise it so it can no longer be linked to you.

6. When we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following situations:

  • Service providers. We may share information with trusted third parties who help us operate the site, process payments, send emails, or provide analytics. These providers are required to handle your information securely and only for the purposes we specify.
  • Performers and show partners. As described in the next section, we share relevant booking and contact details with performers so they can deliver your show.
  • Legal, protection, and safety. We may share information when we believe it is necessary to comply with the law, respond to legal requests, protect our rights or property, or protect you or others from harm.
  • Business transfers. If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or other business transaction, information may be transferred as part of that process, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections.

7. Sharing with performers

Our service connects event organisers with performers. To make this work, we share certain information with performers and, where relevant, their agents or teams.

  • Booking details. For confirmed bookings, we may share the event type, date and time, venue address, guest count, your name, and your contact details so the performer can plan and deliver the show.
  • Quote requests and custom packages. If you submit a QuoteRequest or CustomPackage, we may share the information necessary for performers to prepare a quote or confirm availability.
  • Messages. When you send a PerformerContactMessage, we forward your message, name, and contact email to the performer.

Performers are independent providers and may keep their own records of bookings and communications. They are responsible for their own use of personal information once they receive it. If you have questions about how a specific performer handles your data, please contact them directly or contact us so we can help.

8. Cookies and similar technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember your preferences, and understand how the site is used. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser.

  • Essential cookies. Needed for core functions such as keeping you signed in, maintaining your Cart, or remembering your progress in a booking or package builder flow.
  • Preference cookies. Used to remember choices such as language, previously viewed shows, or whether you have dismissed certain notices.
  • Analytics cookies. Used to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve layout, navigation, and content.

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. If you disable certain cookies, some features, such as saving FavoritesList items or keeping your booking progress, may not work as expected.

9. Email and marketing communication

We use email to complete transactions, to communicate about bookings, and, where permitted, to send you offers or information about new shows.

  • Service emails. These include booking confirmations, reminders, event updates, payment receipts, responses to your questions, and important information about your account or this policy. You generally cannot opt out of service emails while still using the related service.
  • Marketing emails. With your permission, we may send occasional emails about new performers, show packages, gift card promotions, or special offers. You can stop receiving these messages at any time by using the unsubscribe link in the email or by contacting us.
  • Performer communication. When you message a performer or submit a quote request, the performer or their team may contact you using the email address or phone number you provide, for example to confirm details or offer a quote.

If you believe you are receiving unwanted emails from us or from a performer in connection with our service, please let us know using the quick links above or the contact details at the end of this policy.

10. Your privacy rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights in relation to your personal information. These may include the ability to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request that we delete certain information, for example where it is no longer needed for a booking or legal purpose.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing, such as direct marketing.
  • Receive a copy of certain information in a portable format, where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please use the quick links at the top of this page or contact us using the details in the How to contact us section. We may need to verify your identity and confirm details of your request before we can act on it.

If we cannot meet a request because we must keep some information for legal, security, or contractual reasons, we will explain this where possible.

11. International data transfers

Our service may be used by customers and performers in different countries. As a result, your information may be processed in a country that is different from where you live.

When we transfer personal information across borders, we aim to do so in a way that is consistent with applicable data protection laws. The specific protections used may depend on your location and the location of the recipients, and may include contractual safeguards or other appropriate measures.

12. Childrens privacy

Our service is intended to be used by adults arranging events, including events for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children without the consent of a parent or guardian.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us so we can review and, where appropriate, delete the information.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, how we handle information, or legal requirements.

When we make material changes, we will update the Effective date below and, where appropriate, provide additional notice, such as by email or a notice on the site.

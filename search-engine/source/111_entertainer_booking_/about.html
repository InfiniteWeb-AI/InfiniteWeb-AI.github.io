About Magical Revue Booking

Magical Revue Booking is a dedicated booking service for live magic. We connect you with a curated network of professional magicians and show packages so you can add a polished, theatrical experience to your event without the guesswork.

Instead of scrolling endlessly through generic listings, you tell us what you are planning and we translate that into clear options: performers who fit your audience, recommended show formats, and realistic pricing. You stay focused on your guests while we handle the details that make a live show run smoothly.

Every performer in our network is an active professional, used to working in homes, schools, and corporate venues. Our role is to be the bridge between you and the magician: answering questions, setting expectations, and making sure the show that arrives on the day matches what you discussed in advance.

How it works

  1. Step 1

    Share the basics of your event

    Tell us the date you are aiming for, your approximate guest count, and the type of event you are planning (kids birthday, corporate celebration, school assembly, or something else). This gives us enough context to start matching you with appropriate magicians and show formats.

  2. Step 2

    Explore recommended magicians and shows

    Based on your event type, we surface shows and performers that regularly work in similar settings. You can review brief descriptions, typical running times, and starting prices so you understand what a realistic booking looks like before you commit to anything.

  3. Step 3

    Fine-tune details with our team

    When you are close to a decision, we confirm practical details: venue access, sound needs, timing within your schedule, and any special requests. We coordinate directly with the performer so everyone has a shared plan and you are not relaying messages back and forth.

  4. Step 4

    Confirm and enjoy the show

    Once you are happy with the plan, we send a clear confirmation outlining what is included, when the magician will arrive, and any remaining steps. On the day of your event, the show runs to that plan so you can focus on your guests, not logistics.

Events we support

Kids birthday magic shows

For children’s parties, we focus on highly interactive shows that keep young guests laughing and involved from start to finish. Performers tailor their material to your child’s age group, balancing visual magic with simple, funny participation so even shy kids feel comfortable taking part.

Typical kids party bookings include a 30–45 minute show, optional time for photos, and clear guidance on room layout so every child can see the magic. Many performers can also add extras such as balloon twisting or a short “teach-a-trick” segment when appropriate.

Adult birthday & milestone celebrations

For adult birthdays and milestone celebrations, we recommend a mix of close-up magic during drinks and a short, focused feature performance that brings everyone together for a shared “wow” moment. The tone can be light and playful or more sophisticated, depending on your guests.

We work with you to place the show at the right point in the evening so it enhances your schedule rather than interrupting it, and we match you with magicians who are comfortable performing in living rooms, restaurants, or private venues.

Corporate events & client entertainment

For corporate functions, team events, and client receptions, we focus on clean, professional magic that reflects well on your brand. Performers arrive early, dress to your code, and understand how to work around speeches, catering, and AV schedules.

Options range from walk-around magic during networking sessions to structured stage segments for awards nights or all-hands meetings. We help you choose formats that fit your objectives—breaking the ice, rewarding staff, or giving clients a memorable experience.

School assemblies & educational shows

School shows require careful planning: age-appropriate material, an understanding of school schedules, and the ability to hold attention in large spaces like gyms or auditoriums. We work with magicians who regularly perform for schools and youth groups, and who can adapt their material for different grade levels.

Many performers offer themed assemblies—focusing on reading, growth mindset, or anti-bullying messages—where the magic supports what students are learning. We help you align show content with your goals and any school policies around audience participation and media.

Weddings, festivals & other events

Not every event fits a simple label. Whether you are planning a wedding, community festival, fundraiser, or open house, we help you decide how magic can complement what you already have planned.

Together we determine whether you need a roaming magician mingling with guests, a small stage show as a featured attraction, or a combination of both. Our booking team will clarify power needs, sound coverage, and timing so the show meshes cleanly with your program.

Trust & safety

Booking live entertainment can feel intimidating if you have not done it before. Our process is designed to make safety, reliability, and professionalism as transparent as possible before you confirm a show.

We work primarily with performers who are actively working professionals and who have a track record of successful private and public events. Before adding a magician to our roster, we review references, public reviews where available, and performance history.

For events involving children or schools, we ask performers about any relevant background checks or clearances they hold and share that information so you can ensure it meets your organization’s requirements.

Life happens, but your event still needs a show you can count on. If a performer has an emergency, we use our network to seek a suitable replacement with a similar style and skill level, and we keep you informed at each step.

We also make cancellation and rescheduling terms clear in advance so you know what happens if your plans change, and what options are available if weather or venue issues arise.

We act as a central point of contact so you are never left wondering who is handling what. Before the event, we confirm arrival times, technical needs, performance length, and any special announcements or surprises you have planned.

You receive all key details in writing, and we encourage you to reach out with questions at any point. Our goal is for you to feel fully prepared long before the magician walks on stage.

If you have specific safety or policy questions for your school, company, or venue, we are happy to talk them through before you book.

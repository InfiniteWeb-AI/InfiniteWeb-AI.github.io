How the configuration and quote process works

  1. 1. Choose a machine family. Pick the CNC milling family that best matches your work envelope, axis count, and application.
  2. 2. Configure technical options. On the next page (build_machine.html), you will select spindle, tool changer, coolant, enclosure, and accessory options.
  3. 3. Review pricing. The configurator calculates a live estimate based on your choices, starting from the base machine price.
  4. 4. Submit a quote request. When you are satisfied with your configuration, submit your details to request a formal proposal from LUQUE MACHINES.

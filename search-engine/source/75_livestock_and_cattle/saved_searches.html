About saved searches

Saved searches let you quickly re-run your favorite criteria across all livestock auctions and optionally receive in-site alerts when new matching lots are posted.

Tips

  • Use clear names like 'TX bred heifers < $2,000' so you can recognize searches at a glance.
  • Turn alerts on only for the searches you actively monitor.
  • You can always create new saved searches from the All Auctions page after applying filters.

    Tips for cost estimate questions

    Including a few details helps us give you a clearer estimate, especially if you are uninsured.

    • Describe which teeth or area are involved (for example, upper right molar).
    • Mention any exams, x-rays, or treatment you have already had.
    • If you have a quote from another office, share the total and any procedure codes.
    • Let us know your preferred timing so we can match appointment options.

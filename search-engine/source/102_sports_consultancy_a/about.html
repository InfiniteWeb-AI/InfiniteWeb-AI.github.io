Bookings, sessions & cancellations

DOCIA Sport offers 1:1 consultations, group classes, and multi-week programmes. Most sessions are booked in advance online; in some cases we can also arrange bespoke sessions for teams, clubs, or organisations on request.

Consultations & assessments

Consultation services (such as running technique reviews, strength coaching, nutrition support, and wellbeing coaching) are delivered in pre-defined time slots. Each ConsultationTimeSlot has a clearly indicated duration, price, and location (online, in-person, or hybrid).

  • Payment is normally taken at checkout when you add a consultation to your cart.
  • Full details of the coach, location, and what to prepare are shown before you confirm.

Programmes & challenges

Programmes (multi-week plans or courses) and short challenges are scheduled around clear start dates. Depending on the delivery format, you may work self-paced, as part of a cohort, or in a hybrid model with regular live touchpoints.

  • Programme places are confirmed once your order has been completed.
  • Access details are emailed ahead of your chosen start date.

Cancellations & changes

We understand that plans sometimes change. Our terms of service set out the full cancellation and rescheduling policy for consultations, classes, and memberships.

  • Where possible, we ask for at least 24–48 hours' notice for 1:1 consultation changes.
  • Late cancellations or non-attendance may be charged in full, particularly for high-demand time slots and group sessions.

If you are unsure which service is most appropriate, please use the contact section below to tell us about your goals and constraints, and we will advise on the best next step.

Terms of service

These terms summarise how DOCIA Sport provides consultations, programmes, classes, and memberships. They sit alongside any service-specific information shown at checkout.

Bookings & payments

When you confirm a booking or purchase, you agree to the price and service description shown at that point in time. Orders are only confirmed once payment has been successfully received and you have received an order or booking confirmation.

Health & readiness

By participating in DOCIA Sport services, you confirm that you are medically fit to take part and that you will follow any safety guidance provided by your coach or trainer. We recommend seeking medical advice before starting a new programme if you have any concerns.

Changes to services

We may occasionally need to amend session times, locations, or assigned coaches (for example, due to illness or facility issues). Where this happens, we will communicate alternative options or offer a reasonable refund or credit as set out in the full terms.

This is a summary only. The full terms of service, including details on cancellations, refunds, memberships, and limitations of liability, are available on request.

Privacy & data protection

We take your privacy seriously. This summary explains how we handle personal information when you browse our site, make bookings, or contact us for support.

What we collect

Depending on how you use DOCIA Sport, we may collect contact details (such as your name, email address, and phone number), booking and purchase information, basic health or training background you choose to share with your coach, and usage data about visits to our website.

How we use your data

We use this information to provide and improve our services, manage bookings and payments, respond to enquiries, and share relevant updates (for example, changes to a scheduled session). We do not sell your personal data.

Contact forms & enquiries

Messages submitted through our contact form are stored securely and accessed only by staff who need them to respond to your enquiry. We may retain enquiry records for a reasonable period to support continuity of care and service quality.

For detailed information about your rights, data retention, and how to request access or deletion, please refer to the full privacy policy, available on request.

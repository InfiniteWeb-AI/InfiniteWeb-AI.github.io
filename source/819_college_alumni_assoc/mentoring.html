Mentoring Program

Connect students and alumni through one-to-one mentoring.

Our mentoring program pairs current students and recent graduates with alumni who are willing to share their time, experience, and perspective. Use this page to indicate whether you would like to find a mentor or become a mentor.

In Find a Mentor mode, you will be guided to the alumni directory to search for potential mentors. In Become a Mentor mode, you can create a mentor profile so students and fellow alumni can discover and contact you based on your field, interests, and availability.

How to find a mentor

Use the alumni directory to search for potential mentors by industry, class year, location, or keywords in their profiles. When you find someone whose background aligns with your goals, you can send a connection message directly from their profile.

  • Filter by industry or field of study to see alumni in areas you are exploring.
  • Use graduation year filters to find alumni who are closer to your stage or more experienced.
  • Read profile bios to understand each alum’s experience and mentoring interests.

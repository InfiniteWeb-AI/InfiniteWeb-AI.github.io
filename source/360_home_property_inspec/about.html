About Our Inspection Company

Property Inspection Services is an independent, locally owned firm focused solely on residential inspections. Our inspectors spend every day in the field, documenting conditions so that buyers and sellers can make clear, informed decisions.

We follow established standards of practice, provide written digital reports, and encourage clients to attend the inspection whenever possible. Our goal is to explain what we see in plain language, not to overwhelm you with jargon.

Because our team is built around full-time inspectors, we can offer consistent quality across different neighborhoods and property types. Each inspector covers a defined set of ZIP codes so that travel time stays reasonable and appointment times remain dependable.

Services & inspection packages

Notification Settings

Control how Rebirth Online keeps you informed about news, events, and service updates.

Last updated: —

Notification Categories

Choose which types of in-game news and updates you want to be notified about.

  • Server Status

    Outages, disruptions, and live service incidents.

  • Maintenance

    Scheduled downtime and maintenance windows.

  • Patch Notes

    Balance updates, new features, and bug fixes.

  • Esports News

    Tournaments, competitions, and esports announcements.

  • Promotional Offers

    Limited-time offers, bundles, and in-game promotions.

  • Community Stories

    Highlights from the community and featured stories.

  • Guides & Tips

    New player guides, strategy breakdowns, and tips.

Delivery Methods

Choose where we send your notifications. You can enable more than one channel.

  • In-site notifications

    Alerts inside the Rebirth Online news site.

  • Email

    Messages sent to your linked email address.

  • Mobile push

    Push notifications from the Rebirth Online mobile app.

Quiet Hours

Set a daily time window when non-critical notifications are muted.

When quiet hours are enabled, most notifications will be held until your quiet window ends. Critical server status and maintenance alerts may still appear.

How to read these prices

  • registration_price_per_year is the base cost to register a new domain under a given TLD for one year.
  • renewal_price_per_year is what you pay each year to keep an existing registration active.
  • transfer_price applies when you move a domain from another registrar into this account; depending on registry rules, it can also extend your registration term.
  • For add-ons, price_per_year is billed according to the billing_period value (for example, per_year) and usually aligns with your domain's renewal date.
  • All values are shown in the currency field for each row. Any applicable taxes or fees are added during checkout.

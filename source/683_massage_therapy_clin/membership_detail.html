Membership FAQs

Your membership is billed on a recurring monthly basis to the card you keep on file with our clinic. The billing date is typically the day you enroll. Any changes to your billing details should be made before your next billing date.

Memberships can usually be paused or cancelled with advance notice. Specific notice periods and any fees are outlined in the membership agreement you receive at enrollment.

Many memberships allow unused sessions to roll over for a limited period while your account remains active. Rollover limits and expiration dates are detailed in your membership terms.

Some plans are for a single member only, while others allow limited sharing with family members or a designated partner. Sharing rules are included in the plan overview you receive upon enrollment.

Once enrolled, simply book online or by phone and let us know you’re a member. Your included sessions are applied automatically according to the plan you selected.

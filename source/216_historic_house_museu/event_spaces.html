Planning a wedding at Historic Avenel

Use the filters above to match spaces to your guest count, season, and budget. Select Weddings as your event type, choose your preferred month and day of week, and adjust the maximum rental budget to explore ceremony and reception options.

Corporate retreats and meetings

For retreats, trainings, or off-site meetings, select Corporate events, set your expected staff size, and enable the Audio / visual option to find rooms with built-in AV, projectors, or screens. Weekday daytime filters are helpful for retreat pricing.

Social celebrations and other gatherings

For birthdays, anniversaries, and other social occasions, select Social & celebrations. Adjust your guest count and season, and browse spaces that offer the mix of indoor and outdoor settings that fits your plans.

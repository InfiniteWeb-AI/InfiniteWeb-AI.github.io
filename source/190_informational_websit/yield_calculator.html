Legal and risk disclaimers

The yield calculator is an educational tool and is not a promise, offer, or guarantee of any specific return. All figures are simplified estimates based on the inputs you provide and the annual percentage yield associated with the selected stablecoin at the time of simulation.

Actual results may differ substantially due to factors such as changes in interest rates, platform terms, liquidity conditions, counterparty risk, smart contract risk, and market volatility. Stablecoins can lose their peg or become illiquid, and platforms that offer yield may fail or change their reward structures without notice.

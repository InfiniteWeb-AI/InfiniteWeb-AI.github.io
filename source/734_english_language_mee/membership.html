What your membership includes

Every plan connects you with regular English meetups and focused learning time. Choose the structure and support that fits how you like to practice.

  • Meetups: join weekly conversation groups, themed sessions, and social events for real-life speaking practice.
  • Courses: access member-only discounts on workshops and multi-week courses so you can go deeper on speaking, grammar, and exam skills.
  • Community: connect with other learners, follow your progress, and get updates about new events that match your level and interests.

Commissions

I work on a small number of commissioned textile pieces each season. The form below gathers the practical details I need to understand your project: what the piece is for, a sense of scale, your budget, timing, and how to reach you.

After you submit a request, I'll review it and respond personally, usually within 3-5 business days, to clarify details, share a rough quote, and outline next steps.

You don't need perfect measurements or final language yetjust a clear sense of intent. You can always refine the brief with me later in the email thread.

Fields marked with * are required. I use your budget, size, and deadline to plan whether I can take the commission on and how to schedule it alongside existing work.

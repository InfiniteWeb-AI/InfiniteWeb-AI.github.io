Storage services for home and office moves

Use storage on its own or as part of your move. We record your basic requirements here and then show storage units that match on the results page.

Storage is typically used alongside a home or office move when move dates do not line up, when there is limited space at the destination, or when part of the contents needs to be held back for a period of time. Units are available in a range of sizes, measured in square feet.

For combined move and storage jobs, our removals team can load from your property, deliver directly into a storage facility, and later move items out again to your new home or office. This keeps handling steps simple and ensures the correct volume is reserved.

On the next page, we will show storage units that meet the postcode, minimum size and maximum distance you enter below. Pricing and availability are always shown per unit, so you can compare options clearly before confirming any booking.

Typical storage uses

  • Short-term storage between sale and completion dates
  • Overflow space while refurbishing a property
  • Holding archived documents or seasonal stock for offices
  • Storing furniture while downsizing or reorganising

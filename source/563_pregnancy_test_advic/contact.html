Non-urgent questions & safety

This contact form is for non-urgent questions about pregnancy tests, website feedback, or reporting a technical issue.

Do not use this form for urgent medical concerns or emergencies. If you have heavy bleeding, severe pain, feel very unwell, or are worried about your immediate safety, contact your healthcare provider, a nurse advice line, or local emergency services right away.

Other ways to get help

  • If you are in crisis or worried about your immediate safety, contact local emergency services or a trusted crisis hotline in your area.

        Working together – quick questions

        What kinds of projects do you take on?

        Most of my work centres on children's books and character-led illustration, but I occasionally take on editorial pieces and cover illustration when they fit my schedule and style.

        How far in advance should I reach out?

        For full picture books, please get in touch at least 3-1 months before your ideal launch date. For smaller projects (spot illustrations, covers), a few months' notice is usually enough.

        Do you work with self-publishing authors?

        Yes. I regularly collaborate with self-publishing authors and can help you think through page counts, formats, and common production questions.

        Ready to talk about your story?

        Share a few details about your project and we'll find the right way to collaborate.

General feedback (non-grievance)

For suggestions, compliments, or general feedback that is not a formal grievance, you may use the following channels:

  • Share suggestions during handloom workshops, webinars, and outreach activities organised by the Ministry of Textiles.
  • Provide feedback when interacting with Weavers Service Centres and other handloom-related offices in your state.
  • Use any feedback forms or email addresses mentioned in scheme guidelines or official communications.

Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information in general feedback. For issues requiring action or tracking, use the online grievance form on this page so that a formal reference number can be generated.

Support information

If you run into urgent data integrity issues (for example, a game score that affects live rankings), please include as much detail as possible in your message, including sport, teams, and date.

We review most requests within 1–2 business days. For general questions or feature suggestions, response times may be longer during peak season.

If you are unable to use this form for any reason, you can email our support team directly at support@collegesportsratings.example.

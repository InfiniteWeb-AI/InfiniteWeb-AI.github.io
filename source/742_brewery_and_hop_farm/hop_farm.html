Hop Farm & Hops

Quartz Hop Farm is our working hop yard on glacial till soil, just down the road from the brewery. We grow modern aroma cultivars alongside classic bittering workhorses, letting cool nights lock in oil expression and harvest-day freshness.

The hops you see below are the same lots we brew with: picked at peak ripeness, kilned low and slow, and pelletized on a short timeline to protect thiols and delicate aromatics. Each listing notes harvest year, dominant aroma notes, and pricing for homebrew-sized packs.

Pickup & Shipping for Hops

Hop purchases happen on the individual product pages. Once you select your variety and pack size, you will see available fulfillment options at checkout.

  • On-farm pickup: During harvest season we offer pickup at the hop yard on designated days. Orders placed online are packed the same day they are pulled from cold storage.
  • Taproom pickup: Outside of harvest, most hop orders can be held at the brewery taproom for convenience with your next visit.
  • Shipping: Where allowed, hops ship in insulated mailers with ice packs during hot weather. Transit time and rates are shown at checkout.

If you are ordering larger volumes for a brewery or commercial project, start with the varieties below and reach out via the Visit Us page for current contract and spot availability.

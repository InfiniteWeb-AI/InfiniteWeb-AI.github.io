How these gift cards work

Gift cards purchased on this site act as flexible credit that can be applied toward a wide range of music services. The exact way a gift card is redeemed is chosen when the recipient books or purchases their service.

Depending on availability and fit, a gift card may be applied toward:

  • One-on-one lessons with an instructor for instruments such as guitar, piano, voice, drums, violin, ukulele, or other options.
  • Multi-lesson lesson packages that bundle several private lessons together.
  • Group classes or workshops for kids, teens, adults, or all-ages groups.
  • Ongoing subscription plans or memberships that include practice materials or online resources.
  • Selected performance packages for events such as weddings, parties, or corporate gatherings, when offered.

When a recipient is ready to use their gift card, they'll choose a specific service (such as a LessonPackage, GroupClass, SubscriptionPlan, PerformancePackage, or individual LessonBooking) and apply their gift card balance during checkout or coordination. Any service-specific details, scheduling, and policies are handled on the relevant booking or purchase pages.

If you or your recipient ever have questions about how to apply a gift card to a particular service, you can always reach out using the contact options on this site for personalized guidance.

Accessibility

Visitor FAQ

Most visitors spend 60–90 minutes in the galleries. For timed tickets or scheduled tours, arriving 10–15 minutes early allows time for check-in and storing belongings.

All public areas of the gallery are step-free or served by lifts. Accessible restrooms are available, and many guided tours follow wheelchair-accessible routes. See the accessibility summary above or contact us with specific needs.

General admission policies vary by exhibition. Many displays are free or donation-based, while some special exhibitions and tours are ticketed. Check the Admission and Tickets pages for current details.

Spreads & Fees

Review typical spreads, commissions, swaps, and margin requirements across tradable instruments.

This page provides a reference for current trading costs and margin requirements by market category and instrument. Use the filters, search, and sorting below to focus on the instruments that matter to you, and share the URL to preserve the same configuration.

Understanding spreads, commissions, swaps, and margin

Spread is the difference between bid and ask prices and is shown here as typical_spread_pips for each instrument. It contributes to the cost of opening and closing trades.

Commission is listed as commission_per_lot where applicable and usually applies per side for each full lot traded.

Overnight swaps are financing adjustments for positions held past the daily cut-off. They are shown as swap_long and swap_short per lot and can be positive or negative.

Margin requirement is the proportion of notional value that must be set aside as collateral and appears as margin_requirement_percent per instrument and per category. It is closely linked to the max_leverage values in the category summaries.

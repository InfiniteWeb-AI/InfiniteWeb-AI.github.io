Every donation helps us share live choral music, mentor young singers, and keep our concerts accessible to all.

How your gift makes a difference

Your support covers rehearsal space, music scores, guest artists, and scholarships for singers who need financial assistance. Designating a fund lets you direct your gift to the area that matters most to you.

Recognition names are used in concert programs, annual reports, or online acknowledgments when applicable. If you prefer to give anonymously, simply enter “Anonymous” or leave the recognition field blank.

Staying in touch

  • We’ll send an email receipt for every gift.
  • You can opt out of postal mail while still receiving important donation confirmations by email.
  • We send a small number of fundraising updates each season to share the impact of your support.

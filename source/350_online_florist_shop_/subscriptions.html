How subscriptions work

Most plans let you choose how often you receive flowers:

  • every_week – a fresh bouquet delivered once a week.
  • every_2_weeks – a new arrangement every other week.
  • every_month – a seasonal selection once a month.

You can confirm or adjust your frequency on each plan’s detail page.

Once your subscription starts, deliveries follow the cadence you select. For example, an every_week plan delivers once a week on your chosen day (such as friday). An every_month plan delivers around the same date each month.

You’ll see available delivery days and calendar options when you configure each subscription.

Subscriptions are flexible. Before each upcoming delivery you can usually:

  • Skip a single delivery.
  • Pause your subscription for a period.
  • Update delivery details like address or contact info.

Cancellation policies and exact options are shown on the plan detail and in your account after you start a subscription.

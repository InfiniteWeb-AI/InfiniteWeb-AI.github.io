Care Roadmap Planner

Select what matters most, choose tasks, and schedule gentle reminders.

Step 1

Who is this roadmap for?

Choose the role that best describes you. This shapes the suggestions you see.

Step 2

What would you like to focus on right now?

Select one or more priorities. We will suggest tasks that match what you choose.

Step 3

Choose suggested tasks

We've suggested tasks based on your role and priorities. Select the ones that feel helpful.

Loading suggested tasks

Add your own task

If there is something specific you want to remember, add it here.

Step 4

Schedule your tasks & reminders

Set dates and gentle reminders. You can change these anytime.

Tips

  • It's okay to start small. A few realistic tasks are better than a long list.
  • You can choose one-time dates or simple patterns like weekly or monthly.
  • Reminders can be turned on or off any time.

Step 5

Name your roadmap

Add an optional title and notes to make this roadmap your own.

Step 6

Your care roadmap

Review your plan. You can mark progress or edit schedules anytime.

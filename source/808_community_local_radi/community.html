Our Community Mission

Why this work matters

Every show, announcement, and bulletin we air is shaped by the people who live here. When you participate, you keep local voices on the air and help neighbors discover resources, events, and music they might otherwise miss.

Have a story, initiative, or community resource to share? Use the Community Bulletin or volunteer with our Street Team to help spread the word.

What counts as a community event?

Concerts, neighborhood meetings, arts workshops, mutual aid fundraisers, and more—if it's public, local, and community-focused, it likely belongs here.

Want to promote something you're organizing? Use the Community Bulletin, and approved items may appear here or on the on-air calendar.

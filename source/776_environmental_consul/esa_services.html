Understanding Environmental Site Assessments

ESAs help quantify environmental risk before you commit to a property transaction or redevelopment plan. The information below provides general guidance; specific lender or regulatory requirements may differ.

  • Acquisition or sale of commercial, industrial, or mixed-use properties.
  • Refinancing or restructuring loans secured by real property.
  • Redevelopment of brownfield or historically industrial sites.
  • Portfolio-level environmental risk screening for investors or lenders.
  • Internal due diligence before major capital planning decisions.

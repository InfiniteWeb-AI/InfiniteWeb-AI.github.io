How this calculator works

The calculator uses typical delivery patterns from our engineering teams. Project type, scope, integrations, and timelines are mapped to effort ranges that are then converted into cost using your selected currency.

  • Integrations include third-party APIs, payment providers, or external systems.
  • Scope bands (e.g., 5-10 pages) reflect overall feature and screen counts rather than strictly static pages.
  • Tighter timelines increase the required team size and therefore the cost.

What happens after you get an estimate

You can either receive the estimate via email or jump straight into a short MVP development consultation. During that call we refine requirements, validate feasibility, and, if relevant, prepare a precise proposal.

  • Walk through assumptions and constraints together.
  • Discuss technology stack and team composition.
  • Decide between fixed-price project or dedicated team engagement models.

Cost & pricing FAQs

Is this estimate binding?

No. The calculator provides a directional estimate based on limited inputs. A detailed scope and technical review are required before any binding proposal or contract is issued.

Which currencies can I use?

Available currencies are loaded dynamically and typically include USD, EUR, GBP and others. You can change the currency at any time and recalculate.

Can I update the configuration after sending an estimate?

Yes. You can recalculate with new parameters at any time. If you request a consultation, we will always use the most recent estimate as a starting point.

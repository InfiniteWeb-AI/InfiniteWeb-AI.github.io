Self-Assessments

About these self-assessments

These brief questionnaires are designed to help you reflect on your mood, anxiety, and related experiences. They can be a useful starting point for a conversation about your mental health.

Results are informational only and are not a diagnosis or a replacement for an evaluation with a licensed mental health professional.

If you are in immediate distress or thinking about harming yourself or others, please seek in-person help or call emergency services right away rather than using an online self-assessment.

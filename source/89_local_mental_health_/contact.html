If you're in crisis or need immediate help

This form is not monitored 24/7 and should not be used for emergencies. We typically review messages during regular office hours.

If you are in immediate danger or thinking of harming yourself or someone else, please call 911 (or your local emergency number) right away.

You can also call or text 988 in the U.S. to reach the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, available 24/7.

You are still welcome to use this form for non-urgent questions related to crisis support or safety planning.

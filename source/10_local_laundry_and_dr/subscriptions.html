Why choose a subscription?

See how a recurring plan compares to one-time orders.

With a subscription, your laundry runs on a predictable schedule instead of ad-hoc bookings. You get a flat recurring charge and included weight each billing period, so there are fewer surprises.

  • Predictable pricing. Pay the same base amount every billing period for your included weight instead of paying per-order.
  • Automatic pickups. We come to you on your regular pickup days, so you do not need to re-book each time.
  • Priority handling. Subscription pickups are planned in advance, helping us keep turnarounds consistent.
  • Fits your laundry load. Choose a plan that matches how much you wash in a typical period – for example, the included weight in your plan on a regular pickup schedule.

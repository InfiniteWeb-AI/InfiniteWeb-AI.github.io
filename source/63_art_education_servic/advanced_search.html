Advanced Article Search

Construct complex article searches using cited references, journal impact thresholds, and subject filters, then browse matching results.

Construct your search

Combine key cited works with journal impact and subject-area filters to identify relevant research on economics and fraud.

Cited references

Add one or more key references (for example, Becker 1968 or Allingham & Sandmo 1972). Articles that cite these works will be prioritized in your search.

Minimum journal impact factor

Leave blank to include journals with any impact factor.

Subject areas

Hold Ctrl (Windows) or Command (Mac) to select multiple subject areas. Leave empty to search across all available areas.

Results per page

Search scope

The scope controls which parts of each article are inspected (for example, titles and abstracts versus full text). It does not change which filters are available.

Tips for cited reference search

  • Use compact reference strings such as Becker 1968 or Allingham & Sandmo 1972.
  • Combine references with the AND/OR setting to tighten or broaden your search.
  • Layer subject areas and impact factor thresholds to focus on high-impact work in specific domains of economics and fraud.

Contact & Feedback

Use this form to ask questions, report issues, or suggest improvements related to the Economics & Fraud Research Observatory.

Please include enough detail for us to understand the question, issue, or suggestion. Do not include sensitive personal data.

What happens after you submit?

Your message is routed to the maintainers of the Economics & Fraud Research Observatory. We use the information you provide to understand the context, reproduce any issues, and decide on appropriate follow-up.

After submission, you will see a reference identifier (ticketId). You can quote this identifier if you contact us again about the same matter.

We aim to review most messages within a few working days. Responses may take longer for complex methodological questions or if additional coordination with researchers is required.

Feedback about data quality, coding, or methodological descriptions helps us improve both the datasets and the accompanying documentation. Reports of technical issues assist us in maintaining stable access to articles, datasets, and visualization tools.

We may aggregate anonymized feedback to identify recurring issues (for example, common problems accessing a particular dataset). We do not publish individual messages or share your contact details outside the Observatory team.

Tips for fraud research alerts

  • Combine topic keywords (e.g., "money laundering") with regions (e.g., "latin_america") in queries.
  • Use weekly frequency for broad topics; immediate alerts for narrow case studies.
  • Keep long-running alerts enabled during a thesis or project period.

Using Quick Contacts

This page keeps the crisis and support numbers you trust in one place so you do not have to search when you are under stress.

  • Tap Call to open your phone or device dialer with the number filled in.
  • Use Copy number to paste the hotline into a text, notes app, or to share with someone else.
  • Add new numbers from the Crisis & Hotlines directory, then return here for faster access.
  • Remove numbers that are not helpful to keep your list short and easy to scan.

You can update this list at any time. It is here to support you when things feel urgent or overwhelming.

Important safety information

If you or someone you are with is in immediate danger, or you think you may hurt yourself or someone else, call your local emergency number right away (for example, 911 in the United States).

  • This website does not provide emergency services and cannot see or respond to urgent messages.
  • Hotlines listed here are operated by independent organizations. Availability can change, and hold times may vary.
  • If a number does not work, try another option on your list or in the Crisis & Hotlines directory.
  • Keep your device charged and, when possible, know your current location in case a responder asks.

If you cannot safely make a call, consider a trusted person you can ask to contact help for you.

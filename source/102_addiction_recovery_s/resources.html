Using these resources

Articles can help you understand addiction and recovery, but they do not replace professional care or emergency support.

  • Use the search to find topics that feel relevant today.
  • Share articles with trusted family, friends, or group members.
  • In a crisis, contact emergency services or a crisis hotline.

Quick Tips

Short reminders you can return to when things feel difficult.

In the moment

  • Pause and take three slow breaths before responding.
  • Step into another room or outside if you feel overwhelmed.
  • Call someone on your support list, even if it is just to say hello.

Looking after yourself

  • Plan one small thing each day that is just for you.
  • Notice and acknowledge any progress, no matter how small.
  • Keep crisis and hotline numbers somewhere easy to see.

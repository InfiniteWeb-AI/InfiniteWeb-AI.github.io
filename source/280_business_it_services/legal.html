Legal & Privacy

This page explains how we collect, use, and protect information on this site, how you may use our content and tools, and the legal disclaimers that apply to pricing, case studies, and events.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information collected through this website, including blog subscriptions, general inquiries, managed IT and security ServiceConsultationRequest forms, pricing contact forms, resource requests, webinar registrations, case study follow-up forms, and article comments.

Information we collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when interacting with this site, including when you:

  • Submit a general Contact form (stored as a general_contact_requests record).
  • Request a consultation for a specific service (stored as a service_consultation_requests record linked to a services entry).
  • Use a pricing calculator and ask us to follow up (stored as a service_pricing_contact_requests record).
  • Request access to a resource such as a template or guide (stored as a resource_requests record linked to a resources entry).
  • Register for a webinar or event (stored as a webinar_registrations record linked to an events entry).
  • Submit a follow-up request from a case study CTA (stored as a case_study_cta_requests record linked to a case_studies entry).
  • Post a comment on a blog article (stored as an article_comments record linked to a blog_articles entry).

Depending on the form, this information may include your name, business email address, company name, role, company size range, approximate monthly IT budget, preferred project timing, and any message or notes you choose to provide.

Technical and usage data

When you browse this site, we may also collect limited technical information for security, diagnostics, and analytics, such as:

  • IP address and approximate location.
  • Browser type, version, and device information.
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs.
  • Interaction data related to tools (for example, pricing calculator inputs), in aggregated or anonymized form when possible.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide the information, resources, or services you request.
  • Schedule consultations, demos, or follow-up calls related to managed IT, security, cloud, or other services described on this site.
  • Send transactional communications related to events or webinars you register for, such as confirmations, reminders, and follow-up materials.
  • Operate, secure, and improve this website and our services, including troubleshooting, analytics, and performance monitoring.
  • Maintain internal records about how our content and tools are used, in order to better align future resources with user needs.

We do not sell your personal information. We may, however, use anonymized or aggregated data derived from your interactions (for example, typical company size ranges using a particular service) for internal reporting and marketing insights.

Legal basis for processing

Where applicable data protection laws require a legal basis for processing, we typically rely on one or more of the following:

  • Contractual necessity when processing is required to respond to a request you make (for example, arranging a consultation).
  • Legitimate interests in operating a secure website, protecting against abuse, and improving our content and services.
  • Consent when you clearly opt in to optional communications or specific uses of your data.

Data storage and retention

We store form submissions and related records in secure systems, typically represented internally as collections such as service_consultation_requests, service_pricing_contact_requests, webinar_registrations, resource_requests, case_study_cta_requests, and general_contact_requests. These records are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy and to comply with applicable laws, professional obligations, and internal retention guidelines.

Access to these systems is restricted to authorized personnel who need the information for legitimate business purposes (for example, sales, support, and security teams).

How we share your information

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who support our operations (such as hosting, analytics, email delivery, and CRM platforms), subject to appropriate data protection obligations.
  • Event partners when co-hosting webinars, conferences, or workshops, and only to the extent needed to manage registration and attendance.
  • Professional advisers (such as legal or security consultants) where necessary to protect our rights or comply with our obligations.
  • Authorities or third parties if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of us, our users, or others.

We do not authorize third parties to use your personal information for their own independent marketing purposes based solely on your interactions with this site.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. If you disable certain cookies, some parts of the site may not function as intended.

Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under data protection laws, such as the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal or contractual requirements.

About the Innovation Hub & Business Networking Platform

You're viewing the About page to learn more about how the Innovation Hub works behind the scenes.

Our mission & vision

The Innovation Hub is a neutral meeting ground for founders, corporates, investors, and experts who want to build what's next. We connect people, programs, and places so ideas can move from first sketch to scaled company.

Our mission is to reduce the friction of innovation: making it easier to meet the right people, access relevant knowledge, and find practical infrastructure for building a business.

We envision a networked ecosystem where startups, scale-ups, and established organizations collaborate in a transparent, repeatable way — through events, mentorship, coworking, and targeted programs that are designed around founders' real needs.

  • Connect builders and decision-makers across industries and stages.
  • Provide structured paths from idea to investment-ready startup.
  • Offer a physical and digital home for innovation-focused teams.

What we offer

We structure our services into a few core pillars so that founders, partners, and teams can quickly find the right next step. Many members combine several of these pillars over time as their needs evolve.

Events & networking

Curated meetups, demo nights, hackathons, conferences, and themed networking sessions bring together founders, operators, mentors, and investors across sectors such as fintech, AI, healthtech, and more.

Coworking & workspace

Flexible desks, private offices, and project rooms give teams a stable base with the energy of a startup community. Plans are designed for individuals, growing teams, and corporate innovation units alike.

You can explore detailed coworking memberships and meeting-room plans any time via the main Coworking section in the top navigation.

Mentorship & expert access

A vetted mentor network covers topics such as fundraising, product, design, growth, and operations. Members can request focused 1:1 sessions, participate in office hours, or join small-group clinics.

To browse the full mentor directory or request a session, use the Mentors section in the main navigation.

Accelerator & innovation programs

Structured programs support startups from pre-seed through multi-stage growth, as well as corporate innovation initiatives. Each program combines mentoring, curriculum, and access to our wider network.

Success stories & impact

The Innovation Hub exists to create tangible outcomes: real products shipped, teams formed, funding rounds closed, and partnerships launched. Below are a few examples of how different members have used the hub.

From first demo to cross-border partnership

A healthtech startup used our coworking space as a base while joining several sector-specific events. After meeting a corporate innovation team at one of our networking evenings, they co-designed a pilot that led to a multi-country deployment within 12 months.

Scaling via an accelerator track

An AI-enabled logistics company entered one of our seed-stage programs to refine their go-to-market strategy. Through weekly mentoring, investor office hours, and a focused demo day, they secured funding and expanded into two new regions while keeping their core team lean.

Corporate teams prototyping like startups

A corporate innovation unit adopted the hub as their offsite workspace, mixing internal workshops with public events. By tapping into our mentor network and community, they validated new product ideas faster and built lasting connections with startups in adjacent spaces.

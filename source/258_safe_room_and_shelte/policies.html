Policies & Legal

Review how SafeGuard Rooms collects data, processes orders, ships safe rooms and shelters, and supports your purchase over time.

Privacy Policy

This section explains what information we collect when you interact with SafeGuard Rooms, and how we use, store, and protect it.

Information we collect

We collect information that you provide directly when you browse our site, request a consultation, request a custom quote, add items to your cart or wishlist, place an order, or contact our team. This may include your name, contact details, project details, shipping address, and order history.

We also collect limited technical data such as device identifiers, browser information, and approximate location derived from your IP address to keep the site secure and improve performance.

How we use information

We use your information to operate and improve our site, fulfill and support your orders, provide consultation and custom design services, communicate with you about your projects, and comply with legal obligations related to building codes and safety standards.

Order and installation information may be associated with your shelter or safe room for warranty, maintenance, and safety recall purposes.

When we share information

We share only the information necessary to deliver our services. This includes installers, shipping carriers, payment processors, and other providers that help us process payments, ship products, or schedule on-site work. These providers are contractually required to protect your information.

We may also share information when required by law, for example in response to valid legal process or to protect the safety of people in severe-weather or emergency situations.

Data security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections, limited internal access, and secure data storage. No system is perfectly secure, but we design our processes to reduce risk and respond quickly to security events.

Your choices

You can request access, updates, or deletion of certain personal information by contacting us using the details in the Legal & Compliance section. Some information must be retained for warranty, safety, or regulatory reasons.

Shipping & Delivery

This section explains how we ship, schedule, and deliver safe rooms and shelters purchased through SafeGuard Rooms.

Shipping methods

Safe rooms and shelters are large, specialized products. We ship using freight carriers, dedicated trucks, or coordinated delivery with our installers. During checkout, available shipping options (such as standard_shipping, expedited_shipping, overnight_shipping, or pickup) are shown based on your location and product selection.

Not all methods are available for every product or location. For example, underground_shelter deliveries may require local installer coordination or on-site equipment.

Lead times

Lead times depend on product configuration, current production schedules, permit requirements, and installer availability. Estimated delivery windows are shown at checkout and in your order confirmation. These are estimates only and may shift due to severe-weather events, supply chain disruptions, or site readiness.

Delivery & installation appointments

For most safe_room and shelter installations, delivery is coordinated with a scheduled installation appointment. You are responsible for ensuring site access, including clearance for trucks, trailers, and equipment, and for confirming any HOA or permitting requirements in advance unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Service areas and access limitations

We serve many regions through our network of installers, but not all ZIP codes are eligible for every product. During checkout or consultation, we will confirm whether your location is within an installer service radius and whether any access constraints (steep grades, low clearances, etc.) apply.

Warranty & Returns

This section outlines warranty coverage for SafeGuard Rooms products and how returns and service requests are handled.

Warranty coverage

Most SafeGuard Rooms products include a limited structural warranty that covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and installation conditions for a defined period stated in your order documentation. Certain accessories (lighting, first_aid, other_accessory) may have separate manufacturer warranties.

Exclusions

Warranty coverage does not extend to damage caused by improper installation, modifications not approved in writing, abuse or misuse, use outside the stated capacity_min_people and capacity_max_people ranges, extreme events beyond design specifications, or failure to follow maintenance and inspection recommendations.

How to request warranty service or returns

If you believe your product may be eligible for warranty service, contact us with your order number, installation details, and photos of the issue. We may coordinate with the original installer or manufacturer to inspect the product and determine an appropriate remedy, which may include repair, replacement, or partial refund as outlined in your warranty terms.

Because safe rooms and shelters are custom and site-integrated, returns are limited. When permitted, return freight, removal, and restocking charges may apply.

Limitations of liability

We are not responsible for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages, including loss of use or loss of property, except where prohibited by law.

How this self-assessment works

This brief tool helps you reflect on how your family lives out shared values in everyday life. You will see one statement at a time and choose the option that best fits your family's experience.

Most families can complete the assessment in about 5–10 minutes. Your responses are used only to generate a summary and a recommended starter guide you can explore next.

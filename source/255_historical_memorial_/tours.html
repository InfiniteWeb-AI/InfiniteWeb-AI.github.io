Guided tours

Guided walks and study visits introduce the layered history of Belchatow and its Jewish community. All tours are led by trained educators and are designed for small groups.

General tours

General tours offer an orientation to the town, the memorial sites, and key moments in Belchatow's history. They are suitable for individual visitors and mixed-age groups seeking a structured introduction.

Thematic tours

Thematic tours focus on a specific topic, such as everyday life in the pre-war town, the history of the ghetto, or post-war reconstruction. They provide more time at selected locations and deeper discussion.

Jewish heritage tours

Jewish heritage tours trace the presence, culture, and destruction of Jewish life in Belchatow. They follow routes connecting former prayer houses, the cemetery, and places linked to deportations and resistance.

Educational visits

Educational tours are tailored for school and university groups. They combine guided walks with workshop elements and discussion, and can be adapted to curriculum needs and age levels.

How we use email & SMS

We use your preferences to decide which event, workshop, and venue updates we send you and how often.

  • You can unsubscribe from any email at any time using the link in the email footer.
  • SMS messages are reserved for time-sensitive updates like door times, cancellations, or major changes.
  • Transactional messages (for ticket orders, guest lists, or VIP bookings) may still be sent even if you opt out of marketing messages.

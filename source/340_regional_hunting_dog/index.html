About Minnesota NAVHDA

Minnesota NAVHDA is a regional chapter of the North American Versatile Hunting Dog Association. We bring together handlers, trainers, and bird dogs of all backgrounds to develop reliable, ethical hunting companions using proven, test-based training methods.

Through structured training programs, volunteer workdays, and formal testing opportunities, members gain access to training grounds, mentorship, and a community that understands both the hunting and dog-development sides of the work. Whether you are preparing for your first Natural Ability test or polishing an experienced dog for advanced work, you will find guidance and practice opportunities here.

New to NAVHDA or versatile hunting breeds? Start by exploring beginner classes and workshops above, then consider a membership for regular access to grounds, events, and a network of experienced handlers.

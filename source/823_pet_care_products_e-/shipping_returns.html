Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping methods & delivery times

We ship most orders within 1–2 business days. At checkout you will see available shipping methods based on your address and the items in your cart. These methods map directly to our internal ShippingMethod configuration so estimates at checkout reflect how your order is actually processed.

How shipping appears at checkout

  • Standard shipping – Backed by ShippingMethod.type = "standard". Typically arrives in 3–7 business days depending on estimated_min_days and estimated_max_days. A shipping cost is shown based on ShippingMethod.cost.
  • Free standard shipping – When you see a free option, it’s powered by a ShippingMethod with type = "free_standard" or is_free = true and may require a minimum order total via min_cart_total_for_free. If your cart no longer qualifies (for example you remove an item), this method disappears or updates to a paid standard option.
  • Expedited shipping – Backed by type = "expedited". Delivers faster (usually 2–3 business days) for an additional cost shown at checkout.
  • Overnight shipping – Backed by type = "overnight" and priced accordingly. Not all items (for example heavy litter or some flea & tick treatments) are eligible.

Your selected method is stored in the checkout session as CheckoutSession.selected_shipping_method_id and used to calculate your Cart.shipping_total and Cart.total.

For more practical examples of how shipping estimates are calculated or why certain options may not appear, you can review our shipping FAQs.

Free shipping eligibility

Throughout the site you may see badges, filters, or checkout options related to free shipping. These indicators are driven by the same rules we use to calculate shipping charges and eligibility.

Product & variant eligibility

  • Product-level flag – When Product.free_shipping_eligible = true, the product is treated as free-shipping-eligible for all variants unless a variant overrides this. Filters like “Free shipping eligible” on category pages rely on this field.
  • Variant-level override – When ProductVariant.free_shipping_eligible is set, that variant’s value takes priority over the product-level flag. This allows, for example, a 5 lb bag of dog food to qualify for free shipping while a heavier 40 lb bag does not.
  • Cart items – During checkout each line may be marked with CartItem.is_free_shipping when it is fully covered by free-shipping rules (for example a free-shipping coupon that targets just toys). This helps explain why some items have no shipping cost while others do.

Shipping methods & cart thresholds

  • Free method configuration – A method with ShippingMethod.is_free = true (often paired with type = "free_standard") is shown at checkout only when its conditions are met.
  • Order minimums – If ShippingMethod.min_cart_total_for_free is set, your Cart.subtotal must reach that minimum (before taxes and after discounts unless noted otherwise) for the free option to appear.
  • Mixed carts – Carts that contain both free-shipping-eligible and non-eligible items may still have a shipping charge. In that case only eligible items are treated as CartItem.is_free_shipping = true, and remaining items contribute to your Cart.shipping_total.

Coupons that include free shipping

  • Free-shipping coupon type – Coupons that provide free shipping use Coupon.discount_type = "free_shipping". These may require a minimum subtotal via Coupon.min_subtotal and can optionally apply only to certain category slugs via Coupon.applicable_category_slugs (for example only dogs or toys).
  • Scope of coverage – Some coupons remove shipping charges for the entire order; others apply only to qualifying categories. The order summary will show which items are covered by the coupon (marked internally via CartItem.is_free_shipping).
  • Combining with free methods – When both a free-shipping coupon and a free shipping method could apply, the system uses the configuration that results in no charge for the covered items. You will still see only clearly-labeled options at checkout.

When you use filters like “Free shipping only” on product listings, they rely primarily on Product.free_shipping_eligible and ProductVariant.free_shipping_eligible so you can easily shop items that are most likely to ship free for your order.

Returns & refunds

We want you and your pets to be happy with every purchase. Most unopened items can be returned within 30 days of delivery, while certain categories have more specific rules for safety and hygiene. Unless otherwise stated, these policies apply to both one-time purchases and the most recent delivery in a subscription.

Food & treats

This section covers products where Product.subcategory is food or dry_food and Product.product_type is dog_food or dog_treat.

  • Unopened bags or cans – Unopened food and treats may generally be returned within 30 days of delivery for a refund to your original payment method.
  • Opened food – For safety, we cannot restock opened food. If there is a quality issue (for example damaged packaging or product concerns), contact support and we will review options which may include a refund, store credit, or replacement.
  • Subscription food orders – For subscriptions, returns apply to the most recent shipped order only. Future cycles can be paused or canceled as described in the subscription section below.

Litter, litter boxes & accessories

This includes items where Product.subcategory is litter, litter_boxes, or litter_accessories, and product types such as cat_litter and litter_box.

  • Cat litter – Unopened litter (including litter_type values like clumping, non_clumping, crystal, or pellet) can typically be returned within 30 days. Opened litter is not returnable unless there is a defect or shipping damage.
  • Litter boxes & accessories – Clean, unused litter boxes, litter accessories, and similar items may be returned within 30 days. Items that show signs of use or damage may be denied for return.

Toys, scratching posts & accessories

This includes Product.subcategory values such as toys, furniture_scratchers, scratching_posts, accessories, and toy_pack bundles where Product.is_toy_pack = true.

  • New condition – Toys and accessories can be returned within 30 days if they are in like-new condition with original tags and packaging.
  • Wear & tear – We cannot accept returns for normal wear from chewing or play. If an item arrives damaged or fails unusually quickly, contact support with photos so we can evaluate a replacement or refund.

Grooming tools & shampoos

This includes grooming products where Product.subcategory or CartItem.subcategory is shampoos_conditioners, clippers_trimmers, grooming_tools, or grooming_supplies, and product types like grooming_clipper, shampoo_conditioner, and grooming_tool.

  • Electrical grooming tools – Clippers and trimmers (including those with power_type values such as corded or cordless) may be returned within 30 days if they are clean and in like-new condition. Manufacturer warranty terms may apply after this window.
  • Shampoos & topical grooming products – Unopened bottles can generally be returned within 30 days. Opened products are only returnable if there is a defect or adverse reaction; please contact support for help.

Flea & tick treatments

Flea & tick products are highly regulated. This includes products where Product.product_type = "flea_tick_treatment" or Product.is_flea_tick_treatment = true, and items in the flea_tick subcategory.

  • Unopened doses – Unopened boxes may be returnable within 30 days in many regions. Local regulations may restrict what we can accept, so please for guidance before sending anything back.
  • Opened or partially used treatments – Typically not eligible for return for safety reasons. If your pet has an adverse reaction, contact your veterinarian immediately and then reach out to us to discuss options.
  • Subscription treatments – For subscription flea & tick deliveries, eligibility generally applies only to unopened, most-recent shipments. Future cycles can be paused or canceled to prevent additional charges.

Waste bags & cleanup

This includes waste_bags products, including those with eco attributes like eco_friendly, biodegradable, or recycled_materials.

  • Unopened packages – Can usually be returned within 30 days.
  • Opened packages – Not returnable unless there is a defect such as improper sealing or incorrect item received.
  • Subscription waste bags – Returns apply to the latest delivery only; future recurring orders can be adjusted in your subscription settings.

Other categories

Items in the other subcategory or with Product.product_type = "other" generally follow the standard 30-day return window, subject to inspection. Some health or safety items may be final sale where indicated on the product page.

Refunds are typically issued back to your original payment method after returned items are received and processed. Original shipping charges may not be refundable unless the return is due to our error (for example incorrect item shipped or damaged in transit).

Subscriptions & recurring deliveries

Many products (such as flea & tick treatments, waste bags, and frequently used food or litter) are Product.subscription_eligible = true and can be scheduled on a recurring basis. When a product or variant supports subscriptions, you will see subscription options alongside the one-time purchase option on the product page.

How subscription shipping works

  • Frequency options – Available frequencies are defined by Product.allowed_subscription_frequencies or ProductVariant.allowed_subscription_frequencies (for example every_1_month, every_3_months, every_4_weeks, or every_12_weeks).
  • Cart & checkout – Subscription lines in your cart are represented with CartItem.purchase_type = "subscription" and a CartItem.subscription_frequency. Shipping charges for the initial order are calculated just like one-time purchases based on the selected ShippingMethod.
  • Future renewals – Each subsequent shipment is treated as a new order using the then-current product prices, shipping methods, and promotions. Free shipping will only apply to a renewal if that individual order meets the rules at the time it is created (for example reaching a free-shipping threshold or using a valid free-shipping coupon).

Changing, pausing, or canceling

  • Changing frequency or quantity – You can adjust the frequency or quantity for subscription-eligible items (such as waste_bags or flea_tick items) up to a short time before the next order is created. Changes affect upcoming orders only, not shipments that have already been processed.
  • Pausing – Pausing a subscription temporarily stops new CartItem.purchase_type = "subscription" orders from being created. No new shipping charges will apply while the subscription is paused.
  • Canceling – Canceling stops future recurring orders. Orders that are already shipped or in progress remain subject to the return rules in the sections above.

Returns for subscription orders

  • Latest shipment – Returns are generally accepted only for the most recent delivered subscription order, following the category-specific rules (for example for food, litter, toys, or flea & tick treatments).
  • Earlier shipments – Older shipments from the same subscription are usually not eligible for return unless there is a documented product defect.
  • Shipping refunds – If a subscription shipment qualifies for free shipping (for example because it reaches the threshold and the chosen ShippingMethod has is_free = true), there is no shipping cost to refund. Otherwise, shipping charges are typically non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

If you are unsure how a change to your subscription will affect shipping charges or return eligibility, please before making the change.

Damaged, incorrect, or missing items

If your order arrives with damaged items, missing products, or something different than what you ordered, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We may request photos or additional information to help us investigate with our carriers and warehouse teams.

  • Damaged in transit – We will work with you to replace damaged items or issue a refund, including applicable shipping costs when the carrier is at fault.
  • Incorrect items – If you receive an incorrect product, we will provide a pre-paid return label where required and send the correct item or issue a refund.
  • Missing packages – If tracking shows delivered but you cannot locate the package, we may ask you to check with neighbors or household members. If it still cannot be found, we will open a trace with the carrier and work on a resolution with you.

For any of these issues, the fastest way to get help is to with your order number and a brief description of the problem.

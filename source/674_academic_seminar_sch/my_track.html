My Track

This page shows your custom multi-day seminar track in date order. You can expand each day, reorder seminars, remove items, and jot down planning notes while you refine your track.

To add more seminars to My Track, visit the Seminar List or Daily Schedule pages and use their "Add to My Track" actions. Notes entered here are kept only for this browser session.

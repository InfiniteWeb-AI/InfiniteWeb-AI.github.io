Replication clusters

Use replication clusters to keep your Stoolap databases highly available across primaries and replicas.

Replication clusters in Stoolap consist of one or more primaries and zero or more replicas. Primaries accept both reads and writes, while replicas are read-only and continuously apply changes from their primary.

  • Design topologies such as 1 primary, 2 replicas or 3 primaries, 3 replicas.
  • Fail over from a failed primary to a healthy replica with minimal downtime.
  • Scale out read traffic horizontally by adding more replicas.

Use the CLI setup tab to generate versioned commands for creating and managing these clusters from the Stoolap CLI.

Pricing & Limits

View pricing information and plan- and region-specific service limits, including storage limits such as maximum database size.

Pricing

Stoolap pricing is based on the selected service plan, storage allocation, and compute resources in each region. All prices below are indicative; refer to your account billing page for exact charges.

Plan overview

Summary of core plans and typical starting prices
Plan Typical use case Starting price (per month)
Free Trial projects, prototypes, and low-volume personal apps. Included at no cost within documented limits.
Standard Production workloads with steady traffic and predictable growth. Pay-as-you-go based on provisioned storage and vCPU capacity.
Premium High-throughput workloads requiring dedicated compute and IOPS. Tiered discounts for sustained usage and reserved capacity.
Enterprise Mission-critical deployments with strict SLAs and custom terms. Contract-based pricing; contact Stoolap sales.

Storage and compute billing

  • Storage is billed based on the maximum allocated size of each database, rounded up to the nearest GB.
  • Compute is billed per provisioned vCPU and memory bundle, regardless of actual utilization.
  • Network egress may incur additional charges depending on region and bandwidth consumed.

Use the Service limits tab to see the exact storage, compute, network, and other limits that apply to each plan-region combination.

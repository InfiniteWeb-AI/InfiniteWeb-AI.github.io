Contact guidelines

Use this form for questions about tomato varieties, corrections to variety data, or general feedback about the site.

We are a small, volunteer-driven project. Responses may take several days during peak gardening season.

  • For data corrections, include the variety name and where you found the corrected information.
  • For general questions, share your climate, space (containers, balcony, garden), and experience level if relevant.
  • We cannot provide personalized medical, legal, or pesticide-application advice.

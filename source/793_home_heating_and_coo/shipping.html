Regional limitations & surcharges

How this appears on products & at checkout

On product pages you may see badges such as "Free shipping" or delivery windows like "Usually delivers in 3-8 business days". These are based on the shipping methods listed above and on product-level attributes such as shipping_free, delivery_min_days, and delivery_max_days from the product model.

When you add items to your cart and proceed to checkout, we use your delivery address to confirm which methods are available in your region, apply any regional surcharges, and calculate the final shipping cost.

The options and estimates shown at checkout always reflect the current shipping methods and rules on this page, and may differ slightly from product-level defaults if your region has specific limitations or surcharges.

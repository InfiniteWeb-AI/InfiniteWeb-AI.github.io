Suppliers & Registration

This page outlines how we work with suppliers across our group and provides a single registration form for prospective partners.

Before you register

  • Ensure your organisation can meet our standards on quality, safety, ethics, and sustainability.
  • Confirm that you are authorised to submit information on behalf of your company.
  • Have key information ready about your primary region of operation and approximate annual revenue range.
  • Use a monitored business email address so we can reach the right contact for any follow-up.

Registration does not constitute a contract or guarantee of business. It enables our procurement teams to review your details and contact you if opportunities arise.

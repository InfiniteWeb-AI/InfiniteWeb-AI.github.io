Custom assay capabilities

Use this page when off-the-shelf genotyping panels do not match your trait targets, germplasm diversity, or throughput needs.

What we can customize

We design targeted SNP assays across major crops including maize, wheat, rice, sorghum, chickpea, barley, soybean, and specialty or regional crops categorized as other.

Typical designs span from 200–2,000 markers for routine marker-assisted selection up to >50,000 markers for genomic selection and diversity panels. We will work with you to balance marker density, budget, and sample throughput.

Typical timelines

Most projects move from design confirmation to first data delivery in 4–12 weeks, depending on marker count, crop complexity, and required validation.

Before you submit a request

  • Target crop and key traits of interest.
  • Desired minimum and maximum marker counts.
  • Turnaround time constraints (in days).
  • Estimated annual sample volume.
  • Primary contact name and email.

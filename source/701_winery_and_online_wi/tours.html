Visit information

Practical details to help you choose a suitable day and time before completing your booking.

Location

Chaves Estate Winery
Rúa da Ribeira, 21
36630 Cambados, Pontevedra
Spain

Free on-site parking is available. The estate is a short drive from Cambados town centre and approximately 45 minutes from Vigo.

Visiting hours

Standard visiting window:

  • Monday – Friday: 10:00 – 18:00
  • Saturday: 11:00 – 19:00
  • Sunday: Closed for regular visits (selected tastings only)

Individual experiences may offer more limited days or start times; please refer to each experience page for exact availability.

Guidelines

  • Please arrive 10–15 minutes before your scheduled start time.
  • Comfortable footwear is recommended for vineyard walks.
  • Guests under the legal drinking age are welcome on designated family-friendly tours; tastings are reserved for adults.
  • If your plans change, update or cancel your booking via the confirmation email whenever possible.

For group visits of more than 12 guests or tailored corporate events, please contact us via the contact page before booking.

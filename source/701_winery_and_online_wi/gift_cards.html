Gift card FAQs

Enter your gift card code during checkout. The value will be applied to your order total before payment.

Validity periods and any expiry rules are shown on each gift card product page and in your confirmation email.

Digital cards are delivered by email, while physical cards are printed and shipped. Both can be redeemed online in our wine shop.

If the order total is less than the gift card balance, the remaining value stays on the card for future purchases, subject to any expiry rules.
